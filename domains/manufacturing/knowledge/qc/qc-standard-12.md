# QC Standard 12: Printer Visual Quality Standard

## Defect Classification

### Minor

- Short white streak below 0.5 mm width that does not affect readability.
- Cosmetic density variation outside critical label zones.

### Major

- White streak larger than 0.5 mm width.
- Streak repeated across more than 20 mm length.
- Barcode or serial text is partially affected but still readable.

### Critical

- Barcode, QR code, safety label, expiry date, or traceability information is unreadable.
- White streak appears on more than two consecutive production samples.
- Defect affects a customer-facing compliance label.

## Containment

1. Stop shipment for affected output.
2. Segregate output from last-known-good sample to current sample.
3. Inspect back to the previous approved QC checkpoint.
4. Record sample photo, machine ID, material batch, operator, and timestamp.

## Inspection Frequency

- First article at every setup.
- Start of each shift.
- After consumable replacement.
- Every 30 minutes during production run.

## Acceptance Criteria

- No white streak in critical text, barcode, or safety label zones.
- No repeat streak on three consecutive samples.
- Final output must match approved master sample under standard lighting.
