# KAIZEN-004: First Article Print Evidence

## Problem

Printer defects often escape when first article inspection is performed informally or without evidence.

## Improvement

Add mandatory first article print evidence to the setup checklist.

## New Checklist Step

1. Operator prints nozzle or density pattern.
2. Operator uploads or attaches sample photo.
3. Supervisor compares sample against QC Standard 12.
4. Production run cannot start until the sample is approved.

## Expected Impact

- Earlier detection of white streaks.
- Better traceability of setup quality.
- Reduced rework and customer-facing label defects.
