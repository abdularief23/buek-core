# HACCP-011: Packaging Contamination Response

## Scope

Applies to food packaging lines when contamination is found on packaging material, seal area, or finished pack exterior.

## Symptoms

- Foreign particle on packaging surface.
- Seal area contamination.
- Repeated contamination after cleaning.
- Packaging material suspected from supplier lot.

## Hazard Classification

- Minor: outer box dust with no product contact.
- Major: contamination near seal area or repeated on consecutive packs.
- Critical: foreign material risk with possible product contact.

## Immediate Containment

1. Stop the affected packaging line.
2. Segregate product from last good check to current finding.
3. Hold supplier packaging lot.
4. Record photo, line, shift, operator, and material batch.
5. Notify QA and sanitation lead.

## Investigation

1. Inspect packaging roll, feeder, sealing jaw, conveyor, and air blower.
2. Review sanitation record and pre-operational inspection.
3. Compare against HACCP critical control point log.
4. Check whether contamination repeats after cleaning.

## Countermeasure

1. Clean and verify packaging contact surfaces.
2. Replace suspected packaging roll.
3. Increase inspection frequency for next 60 minutes.
4. Release only after QA approval.
